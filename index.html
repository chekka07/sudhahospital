About Us

History of SUDHA HOSPITAL

Sudha Hospital is a 40 years old institution dedicated to a betterment of human life through competent and compassionate medical care at affordable prices for common man. Sudha hospital journey started on 6th july 1972 with Dr. Prasad Rao who is a general surgeon in the district of West Godavari with a 10 bedded hospital.

In 1985 the clinic expanded to a 30 bedded hospital with one OT. Later in 2005 Dr. Ravi Sankar also a Surgeon and son of Dr. Prasad Rao and in 2008 Dr. Prashanthi joined the hospital and over the years increased the capacity to 100 beds and also the number of specialties increased which includes GI Surger- les, Orthopaedics, Urology, Paediatrics, Gynaecology & Ob- stetrics and Infertility centre. Recently in 2015, with the addition of a new building the hospital bed strength has increased to 130 beds and new facilities like Cardiac Cath Lab, CT have also been added.
